## Why a Remote Worker Virtual Card Is No Longer Optional

That's the gap a **remote worker virtual card** fills: a single card, issued in a payment-friendly jurisdiction, that accepts the currencies you actually have, works on the platforms you actually use, and doesn't penalize you for being in the wrong country.

## What Actually Matters When Choosing a Remote Worker Virtual Card

Before we get to any specific product, let's get the evaluation framework straight. Most reviews skip this part and dive straight into "here's a card, here's a discount code." That's backwards. You should know what you're optimizing for first.

1. **Issuing jurisdiction and BIN acceptance.** A card issued in Hong Kong or Lithuania will be accepted by a different set of merchants than one issued in the US. For SaaS subscriptions, BIN matters more than marketing copy — a "supported" platform can still decline your card if the BIN doesn't match what their fraud filters expect.

2. **Crypto top-up support, if that's how you get paid.** Look for which networks are supported (TRC20, ERC20, BEP20, C-Chain), the conversion fee, and the minimum top-up. Network choice directly affects gas fees and total cost.

3. **Total effective fee, not just the headline.** A "0% monthly fee" card can still cost you 3% per transaction once you stack top-up + transaction + FX. Always run the math on a $100 spend.

4. **Daily and monthly spending limits.** This is where cheap cards betray you. A $5,000 daily cap is fine for subscriptions. A $50,000 cap is what you need if you're running Google Ads campaigns or paying contractors.

5. **KYC friction.** Some cards need a passport and selfie. Others need full proof of address. For a digital nomad moving every few months, this matters more than people realize.

6. **Region availability.** Many cards exclude the US, Russia, and a long list of sanctioned jurisdictions. Check this before you fall in love with a product.

7. **Platform-specific compatibility.** Does it work on OpenAI, Google Ads, Facebook Ads, AWS, Steam? These are not interchangeable. A card that's great for Netflix might get declined by ChatGPT.

Keep this checklist in mind. We'll measure PokePay against every one of these below.
